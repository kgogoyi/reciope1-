How to make a Steak

Poterhouse Steak Recipe

The Poterhouse Steak is an amazing combination of 2 wonderful cuts of beef and today you will learn how to cook it !

Ingredients

  • Porterhouse Steak
  • Salt
  • Black Pepper
  • Avocado Oil

Steps

  1. Leave the steak out for 20 mins to reach room temperature
  2. Heat your cast iron pan on medium-low heat and pour oil till smoking
  3. Season your steak to your liking right before you place it on the skillet
  4. Press down on the steak so there is great contact betwen the pan and steak
  5. Flip the steak after 5 minutes and repeat for the other side
  6. After 5 minutes on both sides, lower the heat
  7. Add a tablespoon of butter, smashes garlic, and rosemary
  8. Toss the aromatic butter over the steak for 1 minute
  9. Take the steak off the skillet and let rest for 5 minutes before eating

    10. and voila and perfect steak !!!
